About the Comic

The Story

Meet Chris, a man born with extraordinary powers he doesn't fully understand. Chosen by cosmic forces to seal dangerous rifts in reality, he stands as the last defense against invading creatures from beyond our dimension.

His journey begins after the mysterious disappearance of his adoptive father, setting him on a path to discover his true purpose as an Arbiter – a guardian of our reality. But as Chris embraces his destiny, he finds himself hunted by shadowy forces that want him eliminated.

"One man's destiny, the world's last defense."

Meet the Creator

Vinny Andreano

Writer • Creator • Publisher

Vinny Andreano is a passionate storyteller with over a decade of experience in the comic book industry. After working with major publishers, he decided to create his own universe with Arbiter – a story that has been brewing in his mind for years.

"Arbiter represents everything I love about comics – high-stakes action, supernatural elements, and characters facing impossible odds. I'm thrilled to finally share this world with readers and can't wait for you to meet Chris and discover the mysteries of the Arbiters."

Lore & Worldbuilding

"Everything you know about reality is about to be questioned..."

The Arbiters

Throughout history, special individuals have been chosen to serve as Arbiters – guardians tasked with maintaining the barriers between dimensions.

Born with unique energy signatures, Arbiters can sense and seal reality fractures before they become dangerous rifts.

The Breach

Our reality is under constant threat from entities existing in parallel dimensions – creatures that seek to invade and consume our world.

These beings exploit weaknesses in reality's fabric, creating breaches that allow them passage into our dimension.

The Order

A secretive organization that has monitored reality breaches for centuries, identifying and training potential Arbiters.

With the recent increase in breach activity, The Order faces its greatest challenge – and Chris may be their last hope.

The Growing Threat

Recent years have seen an unprecedented increase in reality fractures across the globe. What was once a rare occurrence now happens with alarming frequency, suggesting a coordinated effort from the other side.

As Chris begins to understand his powers, he discovers that someone – or something – is deliberately weakening the barriers between worlds, and the creatures coming through are just the beginning.
